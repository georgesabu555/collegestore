Department Of Computer Science

Department Overview

The Computer Science Department at Our College provides cutting-edge education and research opportunities for students interested in the field of computer science. Our faculty members are experts in various areas such as artificial intelligence, data science, software engineering, and more. The department provides and supports robust environment for teaching and learning through integrated technology for effectiveness and efficiency. It aims at designing, developing programs and training students to perform tasks related design and management in every field of Information Technology. The main objective of the department is to impart a solid basic knowledge, a sound scientific background and widespread technical application skills to ensure immediate entry into the industry. Considering the fact that the Computer Science Graduates are heavily recruited, the department facilitates its students to achieve their apt career in high technology companies.

Programs Offered

  • Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSc): This program covers fundamental concepts in computer science and programming, preparing students for careers in software development and technology.
  • Master of Science in Computer Science (MSc): Our MSc program offers advanced courses in computer science, allowing students to specialize in areas like cybersecurity, machine learning, and computer networks.
  • Ph.D. in Computer Science: The Ph.D. program enables students to conduct in-depth research in various computer science disciplines, contributing to the advancement of knowledge in the field.
