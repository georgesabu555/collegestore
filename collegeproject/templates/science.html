Department Overview

The Science Department at Our College offers a wide array of courses covering various scientific disciplines. Our dedicated faculty and state-of-the-art facilities provide students with hands-on experience and a deep understanding of scientific principles.Togetherness is the special quality of our staff. We all work together to develop overall personality of our students. We strive hard to inculcate high morale and professional standards among our students. We train our students to excel in academics, co-curricular activities, extra curricular activities and to lead a highly disciplined and organised life.To provide excellence in education and an inspiring environment that ignites the young minds. Programs Offered

  • Bachelor of Science (BSc): Our BSc programs cover a range of subjects including Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, and more, preparing students for diverse career paths in scientific research and industry.
  • Master of Science (MSc): The MSc programs allow students to specialize in specific scientific disciplines, providing advanced knowledge and research opportunities in areas like Environmental Science, Biotechnology, and Applied Physics.
  • Ph.D. in Science: The Ph.D. program enables students to conduct groundbreaking research in various scientific fields, contributing to the advancement of knowledge and scientific discovery.
