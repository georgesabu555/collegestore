Department Of Commerce

Department Overview

The Commerce Department offers a wide range of programs designed to prepare students for successful careers in the business world. Our dedicated faculty and comprehensive curriculum provide students with the knowledge and skills needed to excel in various fields of commerce. The Department of Commerce was established in the year 2015 and was upgraded with a post graduate programme with specialization in Finance in 2017. The Department is an approved research center of ABC university with five research supervisors and 37 research scholars. The department has undertaken several major and minor research projects funded by U.G.C. The Dept. publishes a double-blind peer reviewed half yearly journal “Commerce Spectrum” annotated in the Journal of Economic Literature (JEL), American Economic Association and Indexed in Institute for Studies in Industrial Development (ISID), ICSSR, New Delhi.

Courses Offered

  • Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA): This program focuses on business administration and management principles, preparing students for leadership roles in the corporate sector.
  • Bachelor of Commerce (BCom): The BCom program offers a broad understanding of commerce, accounting, finance, and economics, enabling students to pursue diverse career paths in finance, marketing, and more.
  • Master of Business Administration (MBA): Our MBA program provides advanced knowledge in business strategy, organizational behavior, and entrepreneurship, empowering students to lead and innovate in the business world.
