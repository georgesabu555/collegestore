Department Of Malayalam

Department Overview

The Malayalam Department at Our College offers a comprehensive study of the Malayalam language, literature, and culture. Students explore the rich heritage of Malayalam literature, poetry, and drama, gaining a deep understanding of the language and its artistic expressions. It was started with the avowed purpose of spreading awareness among students about the rich history of Malayalam language which was intrinsically connected to its cultural, traditional and literary practices. The department grew in leaps and bounds under the care and nurture bestowed upon it by eminent teachers who were not only academicians but also writers and thinkers who considered it as their duty to impart their boundless enthusiasm for Malayalam language to generations of students. The rich tradition set by them continues even today. The department of Malayalam functions like a second home for its students, for language enthusiasts of the college, and for meritorious students who aspire to carve a niche for themselves in public life. The department bears testimony to the fact that the growth and glory of Malayalam language is safe in the hands of aspiring and dedicated teachers and students.

Programs Offered

  • Bachelor of Arts in Malayalam (BA): Our BA program covers classical and contemporary Malayalam literature, linguistics, and cultural studies, preparing students for careers in teaching, journalism, and literary fields.
  • Master of Arts in Malayalam (MA): The MA program offers advanced courses in Malayalam literature, literary criticism, and research methodologies, allowing students to specialize in specific periods and genres of Malayalam literature.
  • Ph.D. in Malayalam: The Ph.D. program enables students to conduct in-depth research in Malayalam literature, contributing to the preservation and advancement of Malayalam language and culture.
