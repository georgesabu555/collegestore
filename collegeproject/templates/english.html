Department Of English

Department Overview

The English Department at Our College provides students with a rich and diverse education in the study of English language and literature. Our faculty members are passionate about language and literature, offering students the opportunity to explore various genres, periods, and cultural contexts. Envisioning the Department as a centre of academic excellence, social responsibility, value based education, professional expertise and research in English language, literature and translation. Through the courses offered, our students get an exposure to how writers and thinkers from across the world and cultures have used various genres of literary expressions to represent the real and the imaginary. The study of English opens up a world of opportunities for them in the country and abroad, side by side with the infusion of principles and ideas of the literary art. They acquire a plethora of skills in textual analysis, critical thinking and creative expression. It empowers them to apply the knowledge gained and skills acquired in a wide variety of professional situations.

Programs Offered

  • Bachelor of Arts in English (BA): Our BA program covers literature, linguistics, and creative writing, preparing students for careers in writing, teaching, publishing, and more.
  • Master of Arts in English (MA): The MA program offers advanced courses in literature and literary theory, allowing students to specialize in areas like Shakespearean studies, postcolonial literature, and modernist literature.
  • Ph.D. in English: The Ph.D. program enables students to engage in scholarly research, contributing to the field of English studies through in-depth analysis and critical thinking.
